💡 KEY PRINCIPLES
=================

1. Therapeutic alliance is everything
   → Quality of relationship predicts outcomes

2. Center the person's voice
   → Not the app, not the therapist, not the algorithm

3. Honesty over marketing
   → Don't exaggerate, don't hide, maintain trust

4. Respect difference
   → Culture, identity, spirituality, disability, neurodivergence all matter

5. Real help beats convenience
   → We're tools, not replacements
