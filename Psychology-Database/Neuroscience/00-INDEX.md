## 🎯 Priority Applications

### Video Games & Neuroscience
- **Key Pathway:** Ventral Striatum (VS) Dopamine Release
- **Mechanisms:** Reward anticipation, motivation, habit formation
- **Research:** Preservation of reward responsiveness with training
- **Therapeutic Potential:** Cognitive training, learning enhancement

### Marketing & Consumer Behavior
- **Key Neuroimaging:** fMRI (Blood-oxygen-level-dependent signal)
- **Mechanisms:** Brand loyalty, emotional engagement, subconscious processing
- **Applications:** Ad testing, packaging design, pricing perception
- **Limitations:** Cost, lab environment, ethical concerns

### Psychotherapy & PTSD Treatment
- **Key Systems:** Amygdala → Hippocampus → Prefrontal Cortex
- **Mechanisms:** Neuroplasticity, fear extinction, emotional regulation
- **Evidence-Based Treatments:** CBT, CPT, PE, EMDR, Ketamine-assisted
- **Outcomes:** Hippocampal volume recovery, memory improvement
