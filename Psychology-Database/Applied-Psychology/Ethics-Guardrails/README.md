## Quick Access: The 5 Core APA Principles

1. **Beneficence & Nonmaleficence** — Do good; avoid harm
2. **Fidelity & Responsibility** — Build trust; own your conduct
3. **Integrity** — Be honest; don't deceive
4. **Justice** — Ensure equitable access and quality
5. **Respect for Rights & Dignity** — Honor autonomy, privacy, cultural context

## Critical Principle: Do No Harm

Every design decision, feature, and interaction must ask:

**"Could this harm someone?"**

- Psychologically (triggering trauma, increasing anxiety)
- Legally (violating privacy, enabling harm)
- Socially (discriminating, isolating, reducing real-world support)
- Professionally (exceeding competence, misleading about capabilities)

If yes → **don't deploy without mitigation.**
